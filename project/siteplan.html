Color Schema

The color scheme chosen for this site project includes calming and natural colors:

  • Dark Green (#2E8B57): Used for headings and accents to evoke a sense of nature and calm.
  • Light Grey (#F5F5F5): Used as the background color for a clean, easy-to-read layout.

Typography

The typography choices for this site project include:

  • Arial: Used for the body text due to its readability and simplicity.
  • Georgia: Used for headings to give a warm and approachable feel.
